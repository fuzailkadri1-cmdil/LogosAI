# Product Requirements Document: Logos AI

## Overview

### Product Name
Logos AI

### Tagline
AI Voice Receptionist for Canadian Retail

### Problem Statement
Small retail businesses in Canada lose $30-40K annually to missed calls. When staff are busy with in-store customers, calls go unanswered. After hours, customers reaching voicemail rarely call back—resulting in lost orders, frustrated customers, and revenue walking out the door.

### Solution
An AI-powered voice receptionist that answers calls 24/7, handles routine inquiries (order status, store hours), and captures sales opportunities after hours. Deploys in 30 minutes via simple CSV upload—no complex integrations required.

### Target Market
Canadian SMB retail businesses:
- Boutique clothing stores
- Electronics retailers
- Furniture stores
- Specialty food shops
- Gift shops
- 1-3 locations
- 20-40 daily calls

## Features

### Currently Built (Live)

#### 1. AI Call Answering
- Answers inbound calls 24/7
- Natural conversational voice
- Handles "Are you open?" questions
- English language support

#### 2. Order Status (WISMO)
- CSV upload for order data
- Lookup by phone number or order number
- Communicates order status and tracking
- Handles pickup readiness inquiries

#### 3. Escalation Handling
- Transfers to merchant's phone number when AI can't resolve
- Works for complex inquiries, complaints, or caller requests human

#### 4. Merchant Dashboard (Basic)
- View AI resolution rates
- View calls received
- No call recordings or transcripts yet

#### 5. Simple Deployment
- 30-minute setup
- CSV upload (no API integrations)
- Phone number connection
- Same-day go-live

#### 6. Founder-Led Support
- 1-hour response time during pilot
- White-glove onboarding

### Being Built (Pre-Outreach)

#### After-Hours Lead Capture
**Status:** In development - required before pilot outreach

**During Business Hours Flow:**
```
Caller: "Do you have blue cashmere sweaters?"
AI: "Let me connect you with our team. Before I transfer,
     can I get your name in case we get disconnected?"
Caller: "Sarah"
AI: "And you're looking for blue cashmere sweaters -
     any specific size or color?"
Caller: "Medium, navy blue"
AI: "Got it. Transferring you now."
→ Save lead: Sarah, caller phone, "blue cashmere sweater, medium, navy"
→ Transfer to merchant's phone
```

**After-Hours Flow:**
```
Caller: "Do you have blue cashmere sweaters?"
AI: "Thanks for calling! We're currently closed, but I'd love
     to make sure someone helps you first thing. Can I get your name?"
Caller: "Sarah"
AI: "And what are you looking for today?"
Caller: "A blue cashmere sweater in medium"
AI: "Perfect, Sarah. Someone from our team will call you back
     when we open. Is [caller ID number] the best number to reach you?"
Caller: "Yes"
AI: "Great, expect a call from us soon! Thanks for calling [Store Name]."
→ Save lead: Sarah, phone, "blue cashmere sweater, medium", after_hours
→ End call (don't transfer - no one will answer)
```

**Lead Data Captured:**
- caller_name
- caller_phone (from caller ID or provided)
- inquiry (what they're looking for)
- timestamp
- call_type: "during_hours" or "after_hours"
- status: "new"

## Technical Architecture

### Current Stack (Inferred)
- **Hosting:** Replit
- **Voice:** Likely Twilio or similar
- **Data:** CSV upload → internal database
- **Frontend:** Web app at call-center-pilot-fuzailkadri1.replit.app

### Data Flow
```
Merchant uploads CSV (orders)
         ↓
System indexes by phone + order #
         ↓
Customer calls store number
         ↓
Call routes to Logos AI
         ↓
AI answers, identifies intent
         ↓
    ┌────────────────────────────┐
    ↓              ↓             ↓
Order Status    Store Info    Sales Intent
    ↓              ↓             ↓
CSV lookup     Knowledge     Capture lead
    ↓          base query    & notify
    ↓              ↓             ↓
Respond        Respond       Confirm &
with status    with info     follow up
    └────────────────────────────┘
                 ↓
    "Anything else I can help with?"
                 ↓
           End call
```

### CSV Format (Current)
```
order_id, customer_name, customer_phone, status, tracking_number, expected_date
10042, John Smith, +14165551234, shipped, 1Z999AA10123456784, 2026-01-30
10043, Jane Doe, +16045559876, ready_for_pickup, ,
```
