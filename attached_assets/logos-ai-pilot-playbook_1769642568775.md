# Logos AI Pilot Playbook

## Overview

This playbook guides you through running successful 60-day pilots with SMB retail customers. The goal: prove value fast, hit the 60% automation guarantee, and convert to $1,000/month paid customers.

---

## Pilot Success Criteria

### Minimum Bar (Guarantee)
- 60% call automation rate
- If not met → full refund

### Target Outcomes
| Metric | Target | How to Measure |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Automation Rate | 75%+ | Calls resolved by AI / Total calls |
| Revenue Recovered | $3K+/month | Orders attributed to AI-handled calls |
| Staff Time Saved | 10+ hrs/week | Call volume × avg handle time |
| Merchant Satisfaction | 4.5+/5 | End-of-pilot survey |
| Conversion to Paid | Yes | Signs $1K/month contract |

## Pilot Timeline

### Week 0: Pre-Launch (Day -7 to Day 0)

#### Day -7: Discovery Call (30 min)
**Objectives:**
- Understand their business and call patterns
- Identify top 3 call types to automate
- Confirm they're a good fit

**Qualification Checklist:**
- [ ] 20-40 calls/day
- [ ] 1-3 locations
- [ ] Retail business (not services)
- [ ] Has order data they can export
- [ ] Decision maker on call
- [ ] Pain is real (missing calls, losing sales)

**Questions to Ask:**
1. "Walk me through a typical day—when do you miss the most calls?"
2. "What are the top 3 things callers ask about?"
3. "How do you handle after-hours calls today?"
4. "What does a missed call cost you in lost sales?"
5. "How do you track orders today? Can you export to CSV?"

**Red Flags (Don't Pilot):**
- Fewer than 15 calls/day (not enough volume)
- Complex calls requiring expertise (e.g., technical support)
- No order tracking system
- Looking for free trial, not willing to pay

#### Day -3: Onboarding Prep
**Send to Merchant:**
- [ ] CSV template with example data
- [ ] List of info needed (hours, policies, FAQs)
- [ ] Phone number porting instructions (if applicable)
- [ ] Calendly link for setup call

**Prepare Internally:**
- [ ] Create merchant account
- [ ] Set up their phone number routing
- [ ] Prepare knowledge base template

#### Day 0: Setup Call (30-45 min)
**Agenda:**
1. Upload order CSV together (10 min)
2. Configure store hours and basic info (10 min)
3. Record/select greeting and voice (5 min)
4. Test call together (5 min)
5. Go live! (5 min)
6. Set expectations for Week 1 (5 min)

**Go-Live Checklist:**
- [ ] Order CSV uploaded and indexed
- [ ] Store hours configured
- [ ] Return policy added
- [ ] Location/directions added
- [ ] Greeting customized with store name
- [ ] Test call successful
- [ ] Merchant knows how to reach you (support)

---

### Week 1: Stabilization (Days 1-7)

**Your Focus:** Catch problems early, tune the AI, build confidence

#### Daily Tasks
- [ ] Check dashboard for resolution rates and call volume
- [ ] Review any new leads captured (text merchant about after-hours leads)
- [ ] Note any escalation spikes or issues
- [ ] Check in with merchant (quick text/call)
- [ ] Tune AI responses if issues reported

#### Day 1 Check-In (Required)
**Questions:**
- "How did the first day feel?"
- "Any calls that didn't go well?"
- "Any questions from your staff?"

**Common Day 1 Issues:**
| Issue | Fix |
| --- | --- |
| AI mispronouncing store name | Update phonetic spelling |
| Order not found | Check CSV format, phone number formatting |
| Wrong store hours | Update configuration |
| Caller confused by AI | Adjust greeting to set expectations |

#### Day 3 Check-In (Required)
**Review:**
- Call volume vs. expectation
- Automation rate (target: 50%+ by day 3)
- Any patterns in escalated calls

**Adjust:**
- Add FAQs based on real calls
- Update knowledge base gaps
- Retrain AI on problem phrases

#### Day 7 Review (Required)
**Metrics Review:**
- Total calls handled
- Automation rate
- Escalation reasons
- Merchant satisfaction (1-5)

**Week 1 Health Check:**
| Metric | Green | Yellow | Red |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Automation Rate | >50% | 40-50% | <40% |
| Merchant Engagement | Responsive | Slow | Silent |
| Call Quality | No complaints | Minor issues | Major failures |

**If Red:** Schedule emergency call, consider pausing pilot

---

### Weeks 2-4: Optimization (Days 8-30)

**Your Focus:** Hit automation targets, demonstrate value, build case study

#### Weekly Rhythm
- **Monday:** Review prior week metrics
- **Wednesday:** Quick merchant check-in (15 min)
- **Friday:** Update CSV if order data changed

#### Week 2 Goals
- Reach 60% automation rate (guarantee threshold)
- Identify top 3 remaining escalation reasons
- Add 5+ new FAQs based on real calls

#### Week 3 Goals
- Reach 70% automation rate
- Document first revenue recovery story
- Merchant can articulate value in their words

#### Week 4 Goals
- Reach 75% automation rate
- Capture 2-3 specific wins (saved sales, time saved)
- Prep for mid-pilot business review

#### Mid-Pilot Business Review (Day 30)
**Agenda (45 min):**
1. Metrics review (15 min)
  - Calls handled
  - Automation rate
  - Estimated revenue recovered
  - Time saved

2. Review wins together (10 min)
  - Walk through captured leads and their outcomes
  - "Remember that after-hours lead for [product]? Did they buy?"
  - Ask merchant to share a positive caller experience
  - Celebrate wins together

3. Address concerns (10 min)
  - What's not working?
  - What do they wish it did?
  - Any caller complaints or feedback?

4. Preview conversion discussion (10 min)
  - "At day 60, we'll discuss continuing at $1K/month"
  - "Based on what you're seeing, does that feel like good value?"
  - Plant seed, don't push

---

### Weeks 5-8: Value Proof & Conversion (Days 31-60)

**Your Focus:** Lock in results, build case study, close the deal

#### Week 5-6: Solidify Results
- Maintain 75%+ automation rate
- Document 3+ specific revenue recovery stories
- Get merchant comfortable talking about results

#### Week 7: Pre-Conversion Prep
**Internal Prep:**
- [ ] Compile full pilot metrics
- [ ] Calculate ROI (revenue recovered / $500 cost)
- [ ] Prepare case study draft (with permission)
- [ ] Identify any objections to address

**Merchant Prep (Week 7 call):**
- Preview final review meeting
- Ask: "What would make continuing a no-brainer?"
- Address any lingering concerns

#### Day 55-60: Conversion Meeting
**Agenda (30 min):**

1. **Celebrate Results (10 min)**
  - Total calls automated
  - Revenue recovered (with specific examples)
  - Time saved
  - Share 2-3 lead capture success stories

2. **ROI Calculation (5 min)**
```
   Revenue recovered: $4,200/month
   Pilot cost: $500/month
   Production cost: $1,000/month
   Net monthly gain: $3,200/month
   ROI: 320%
```

3. **Conversion Ask (10 min)**
  - "Based on these results, I'd love to continue working together."
  - "The production rate is $1,000/month. Given you're recovering $4K+, this is a 4x return."
  - "Can we get you set up on a monthly plan?"

4. **Handle Objections (5 min)**
   | Objection | Response |
   |-----------|----------|
   | "Price is high" | "You're making $3K+ net. Would you pay $1K to make $3K?" |
   | "Need to think about it" | "Totally understand. What questions can I answer now? Can we reconnect Thursday?" |
   | "Want to try without" | "Let's do a 2-week off period. Track missed calls, we'll compare." |
   | "Seasonal business" | "Let's do month-to-month. Pause in slow season." |

5. **Close or Next Steps**
  - If yes: Send contract, set up billing
  - If maybe: Schedule follow-up within 5 days
  - If no: Ask why, learn, thank them

## Pilot Operations

### CSV Management

**Upload Frequency:** Daily recommended, minimum 2x/week

**CSV Template:**
```csv
order_id,customer_name,customer_phone,status,tracking_number,expected_date
10042,John Smith,+14165551234,shipped,1Z999AA10123456784,2026-01-30
10043,Jane Doe,+16045559876,ready_for_pickup,,
10044,Bob Wilson,+14165557890,processing,,2026-02-02
```

**Status Values:**
- `processing` - Order received, not yet shipped
- `shipped` - In transit, include tracking
- `ready_for_pickup` - Ready at store
- `delivered` - Completed
- `cancelled` - Order cancelled

**Common CSV Issues:**
| Issue | Symptom | Fix |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Phone format wrong | Order not found | Use E.164: +14165551234 |
| Missing header row | Import fails | Add header row |
| Extra spaces | Lookup fails | Trim whitespace |
| Date format wrong | Display issues | Use YYYY-MM-DD |

### Lead Management (Manual During Pilots)

**Current State:** No automated lead notifications. You must manually monitor and alert merchants.

**Daily Lead Review Process:**
1. Check dashboard for new leads captured
2. For each after-hours lead:
  - Text merchant immediately: "New lead from last night: [Name] called looking for [inquiry]. Their number: [phone]. Call them back!"
3. Track which leads convert to sales (ask merchant in check-ins)

**Lead Notification Template (Text to Merchant):**
```
Hey [Merchant Name]! You got an after-hours inquiry:

[Caller Name] called at [time]
Looking for: [inquiry details]
Callback #: [phone number]

Call them back to close the sale!
```

**Why Manual Works for Now:**
- You're running <5 pilots
- Builds relationship with merchants (high-touch)
- Lets you see what's working before automating

### Call Quality Monitoring

**Current State:** No call recordings. Monitor via dashboard metrics and merchant feedback.

**Daily Review Process:**
1. Check dashboard for resolution rates and call volume
2. Note any escalation spikes
3. Ask merchant about call quality in check-ins
4. If issues reported, tune AI prompts

**Proxy Metrics (Without Recordings):**
| Metric | What It Tells You |
| --- | --- |
| Resolution rate dropping | AI struggling with new call types |
| High escalation rate | May need more FAQs or training |
| Merchant complaints | Call quality issues |
| Leads not converting | AI may be capturing incomplete info |

**Escalation Tracking:**
Ask merchants what types of calls are escalating:
- Order issue (refund, cancel, change)
- Product question (do you have X?)
- Complaint
- Requested human
- AI didn't understand
- Other

## Troubleshooting Guide

### "Automation Rate Too Low"

**Target:** 60%+ (guarantee), 75%+ (goal)

**Diagnosis:**
1. Pull escalation reasons
2. Identify top 3 causes
3. Address each:

| Cause | Fix |
| --- | --- |
| Order not found | Check CSV freshness, phone format |
| Product questions | Add inventory data or FAQ |
| AI didn't understand | Ask merchant for examples, tune AI prompts |
| Callers want human | Adjust greeting, set expectations |
| Complex order issues | Expected - these should escalate |

### "Merchant Unresponsive"

**Day 1-3:** Text + call, emphasize importance of early tuning
**Day 4-7:** Email summary of what you're seeing, ask for 10 min
**Day 8+:** Send metrics, ask if they want to continue or pause

**If No Response by Day 14:**
- Call to discuss pausing pilot
- Offer to restart when they have bandwidth
- Don't let ghost pilots drag on

### "Caller Complaints"

**Immediate:**
1. Get details from merchant (what did caller say happened?)
2. Check dashboard for that call's outcome
3. Identify likely cause based on call type
4. Fix the issue (AI prompt, FAQ, data)
5. Respond to merchant within 1 hour

**Follow-Up:**
1. Ask merchant to flag similar complaints
2. Monitor resolution rates for that call type
3. Update AI training if pattern emerges
4. Report back to merchant with fix

### "Merchant Wants to Cancel"

**Before Day 30:**
- Understand why
- Offer to pause and restart
- If quality issue, offer 2 weeks free to fix
- If fit issue, let them go gracefully

**After Day 30:**
- Remind of results so far
- Offer to complete pilot at no further cost
- Ask for honest feedback
- Request referral if positive experience despite cancellation
