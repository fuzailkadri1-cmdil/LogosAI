Demo Script for Investor Presentations

Follow this 5-10 minute script to showcase your call center automation platform effectively.

Pro Tip: Customize this script based on your audience. For technical investors, emphasize the AI and architecture. For industry-focused investors, highlight the ROI and market opportunity.
1. Opening Hook (30 seconds)
What to Say:
"American businesses waste over $40 billion annually on repetitive customer support calls. The average call center spends $5-15 per call on questions like 'What's my order status?' or 'What are your hours?'

We automate 70% of these calls with voice AI, saving companies thousands per month while improving customer experience."
Action:

Open the ROI Calculator and plug in real numbers:

  • 1,000 calls/month × 5 minutes × $15/hour = $1,250/month cost
  • 70% automation = $875/month savings → $10,500/year
2. Problem → Solution (2 minutes)
What to Say:
"Traditional IVR systems are frustrating. Press 1 for this, press 2 for that. Customers hate them, and they still transfer 50% of calls to human agents.

Our platform uses conversational AI that actually understands what callers want—whether they say 'track my package' or 'where's my order?'—no rigid menus."
Action:

Walk through the landing page:

  • Show the conversational UI examples (eCommerce, Healthcare, Transportation)
  • Point out industry-specific pain points
  • Emphasize "This works across Twilio, Cisco, and any VoIP provider"
3. Live Product Demo (5 minutes)
What to Say:
"Let me show you how easy it is to set up and use. We'll walk through the actual platform."
Step 1: Conversation Preview (1 min)

Open Conversation Preview

  • Type: "What's my order status for order #12345?"
  • Point out: "Notice it detected 'OrderStatus' intent without the caller pressing any buttons"
  • Type: "What are your store hours?"
  • Point out: "Instant response, no wait time, no agent needed"
Step 2: Listen Mode Analytics (2 min)

Open Listen Mode

  • Say: "This is where the AI learns. It analyzes call patterns and recommends which intents to automate."
  • Point to high-volume intents: "See this? 150 calls asking about order status. That's an easy automation."
  • Show automation potential: "We estimate 85% of these could be handled by AI."
Step 3: Industry Templates (1 min)

Open Industry Templates

  • Say: "A healthcare clinic can go live in 5 minutes with our pre-built templates."
  • Click on "Healthcare" template
  • Show the pre-configured greetings and menu options
  • Optionally: Click "Apply Template" to show instant setup
Step 4: Dashboard Overview (1 min)

Open Dashboard

  • Point out automation rate, cost savings, and call volume
  • Say: "Every company gets real-time visibility into exactly what their AI is doing."
4. Market Opportunity (1-2 minutes)
What to Say:
"The global call center market is $339 billion and growing. We're targeting small-to-medium businesses—10 to 500 employees—who can't afford custom enterprise solutions.

Our initial verticals are eCommerce, Healthcare, and Transportation because they have the highest call volumes and clearest ROI."
Action:

Open Investor Dashboard to show:

  • TAM/SAM/SOM breakdown
  • Competitive positioning (vs. Dialpad, Zendesk Talk, traditional IVR)
  • Unit economics and growth projections
5. Closing & Ask (30 seconds)
What to Say:
"We've built this MVP in [X weeks/months] and we're ready to onboard our first 10 customers.

We're raising $500K to:
  • Hire 2 engineers to build advanced AI features
  • Launch integrations with Shopify, Salesforce, and Zendesk
  • Acquire our first 50 paying customers through targeted marketing
Are you interested in leading or participating in this round?"
6. Common Investor Questions & Answers
Q: How is this different from existing IVR systems?

A: Traditional IVR uses rigid menu trees (press 1, press 2). We use conversational AI that understands natural language—whether someone says "track my order" or "where's my package," it works.

Q: What about competitors like Dialpad or Five9?

A: They're enterprise-focused with 6-figure contracts and long setup times. We're SMB-focused, self-serve, and customers can launch in under 10 minutes. Our starting price is $99/month vs. their $50K+ annual contracts.

Q: How do you make money?

A: SaaS subscription model with 3 tiers: Starter ($99/mo), Professional ($299/mo), Enterprise (custom). Average customer LTV is $10,800 over 3 years with CAC under $500.

Q: Why will customers switch from their current system?

A: ROI is clear and immediate. If a business handles 1,000 calls/month, we save them $10K+ annually. Plus, setup is 10 minutes vs. weeks with legacy systems.

Q: What's your go-to-market strategy?

A: 1) Content marketing targeting "reduce call center costs" searches, 2) Partnerships with Shopify/WooCommerce app stores, 3) Outbound to mid-market eCommerce brands with >10K monthly orders.

Q: What are the technical risks?

A: Voice AI accuracy is our biggest risk. Currently at 85% intent detection accuracy. We're planning OpenAI integration to boost this to 95%+. We also have fallback to human agents for edge cases.
