Logos AI - Investor Demo Script

SMB-First AI Phone System for Retail & eCommerce

Follow this 5-10 minute script to showcase Logos AI effectively to investors.

Key Message: We're honest about what our prototype does today (retail/eCommerce order status, store hours, FAQs) versus what's on our roadmap. Investors appreciate transparency.
Technical Investor Talking Points
AI Architecture
  • GPT-4o-mini integration: "We use OpenAI's efficient model for natural language understanding"
  • Intent detection: "Two-stage system: keyword matching first, then LLM for complex queries"
  • Speech normalization: "We handle Twilio's varied transcriptions robustly"
Current vs Future
  • Now CSV order upload, 4 intents, Twilio integration
  • Q1 2025 Shopify integration, Stripe integration
  • Q2 2025 Healthcare & transportation verticals
1. Opening - The Problem (30 seconds)
"SMB retail and eCommerce businesses waste 30-50% of their support time on repetitive calls: 'Where's my order?' 'What are your store hours?' 'Is my pickup ready?'

Enterprise solutions cost $50K+ and take months to implement. Small businesses are stuck with expensive manual support or frustrating IVR menus."
2. Our Solution - Logos AI (1 minute)
"Logos AI is an AI phone system built specifically for SMB retail and eCommerce. We handle the repetitive calls automatically and escalate complex issues to humans.

What makes us different:
  • Honest about limits: We automate what we can, escalate what we can't
  • Simple setup: Upload your order CSV, configure your greeting, go live in 1 day
  • SMB pricing: $99/month pilot program, not $50K enterprise contracts
"
3. Live Demo (3-5 minutes)
Show Working Prototype
  1. Open Conversation Preview
  2. Type: "What's my order status for order #12345?"
  3. Show AI response with order details
  4. Type: "What are your store hours?"
  5. Type: "I need to speak with someone about a refund"
  6. Point out: "Notice it escalates to human when needed"
Show Dashboard & Analytics
  1. Open Dashboard
  2. Point out: "Real-time call metrics, AI automation rate, intent distribution"
  3. Show call history and transcripts
Show Pilot Management
  1. Open Pilot Customers
  2. "This is how we onboard pilot customers - simple CSV upload"
  3. "Each pilot gets their own phone number and analytics"
4. What Works Today vs Roadmap (1 minute)
Working Now
  • AI order status lookup (via CSV)
  • Store hours & FAQ responses
  • Pickup readiness inquiries
  • Intelligent human handoff
  • Twilio phone integration
  • Analytics dashboard
  • Pilot customer management
On Roadmap
  • Q1 2025: Shopify integration
  • Q1 2025: Stripe integration
  • Q2 2025: Healthcare vertical
  • Q2 2025: Transportation vertical
  • Q3 2025: Salesforce/Zendesk
"We're honest about what works today. This is a prototype ready for pilot customers, not a finished product. But it proves the core technology works."
5. Investment Ask (30 seconds)
"We're raising $100K-$500K to:
  • Build Shopify and Stripe integrations
  • Onboard 10-20 pilot customers
  • Prove 30-50% call reduction ROI
  • Position for Series A
Are you interested in joining our seed round?"
Common Investor Questions
Q: Why focus on retail/eCommerce first?

A: Order status is the #1 inbound call type. It's well-structured data, easy to automate, and proves immediate ROI. Once we nail this vertical, we expand.

Q: What about competitors like Dialpad?

A: They're enterprise-focused with 6-figure contracts. We're SMB-focused with $99/month pricing and 1-day setup.

Q: How do you handle edge cases?

A: We're honest about limits. Complex issues get escalated to humans. We don't try to automate everything - just the 30-50% that's repetitive.

Q: What's your technical moat?

A: Our moat is focus: SMB-first, retail-first, simple setup. Enterprise competitors can't serve this market profitably.
