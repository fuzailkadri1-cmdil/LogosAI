Twilio Setup Guide - Get a Live Demo Number

Follow these steps to get a real phone number that showcases your AI call automation to investors.

Why Twilio? Twilio offers a free trial with $15 credit—enough for ~100 demo calls. You can have a working phone number in under 10 minutes.
Step 1: Create Your Twilio Account
  1. Go to https://www.twilio.com/try-twilio
  2. Sign up with your email (no credit card required for trial)
  3. Verify your email and phone number
  4. You'll receive $15 in free trial credit
This takes about 2 minutes
Step 2: Get Your Twilio Credentials
  1. Log in to your Twilio Console
  2. On the dashboard, find these two values:
    • Account SID - Starts with "AC..."
    • Auth Token - Click "Show" to reveal it
  3. Copy both values—you'll need them in Step 4
Security: Never share your Auth Token publicly. It's like a password for your Twilio account.
Step 3: Buy a Phone Number
  1. In the Twilio Console, go to Phone Numbers → Manage → Buy a number
  2. Select your country (e.g., United States)
  3. Check "Voice" capability
  4. Click Search
  5. Choose any available number and click Buy
  6. Cost: $1/month (deducted from your free credit)
Your phone number is now active!
Step 4: Configure Your Number in This Platform
  1. Go to Onboarding in your dashboard
  2. Select Twilio as your provider
  3. Enter the credentials from Step 2:
    • Account SID
    • Auth Token
    • Phone Number (from Step 3)
  4. Click Save Integration
The platform will automatically validate your credentials.
Step 5: Point Twilio to Your Webhook

This is the most important step—it tells Twilio to send incoming calls to your AI platform.

  1. In Twilio Console, go to Phone Numbers → Manage → Active numbers
  2. Click on your phone number
  3. Scroll down to Voice Configuration
  4. Under "A CALL COMES IN", configure:
    • Webhook: (select this option)
    • URL: Copy the URL below 👇
Your Webhook URL:
Method: HTTP POST
  1. Click Save Configuration
Your Twilio number is now connected to your AI platform!
Step 6: Test Your Setup
Option A: Call Your Number
  1. Use your personal phone to call the Twilio number from Step 3
  2. You should hear your company greeting
  3. Try saying "What's my order status?" or press menu options
  4. The AI should respond with appropriate information
Option B: Use the Dashboard Test
  1. Go to Dashboard
  2. Find your Twilio integration
  3. Click Test Integration
  4. Check for "Success" message
Pro Tip: After making test calls, check Call Logs to see the full transcript and intent detection.
Troubleshooting Common Issues
Issue: Call goes to voicemail immediately

Solution: Check that your webhook URL is correct in Twilio. Make sure it ends with /voice/webhook and uses HTTP POST.

Issue: "Cannot read your Twilio credentials"

Solution: Double-check your Account SID and Auth Token. Make sure there are no extra spaces when copying.

Issue: Call connects but AI doesn't respond

Solution: Verify your company greeting is set in Settings. If blank, the AI won't know what to say.

Issue: "This number cannot accept incoming calls"

Solution: Verify your phone number in Twilio trial. Trial accounts can only call verified numbers unless you upgrade.

Issue: Webhook URL returns 404 error

Solution: Make sure your Replit app is running. Check that the URL includes your full Replit domain.
